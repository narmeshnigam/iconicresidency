Frequently Asked Questions

Is the project government approved?

Yes, Iconic Residency is fully approved and plotted as per urban development norms.

What is the starting price of the plots?

Currently, launch pricing is ₹36,500 per sq. yd. Subject to change post-phase 1.

What plot sizes are available?

We offer a range of sizes from 70 sq. yd to 180 sq. yd with clear demarcation.

What documents will I get after booking?

Buyers receive allotment letter, registry papers, and a copy of layout map.

Is there any site visit facility available?

Yes, free site visit pickup/drop is available on appointment.

Is bank loan facility available?

Loan processing is available through tie-ups and self-financed buyers can also proceed.

How soon can construction begin after purchase?

Immediately post-registry. No holding period from our side.

Is there any maintenance or hidden charge?

There are no recurring maintenance charges for plots. One-time development charges may apply.
